Summary of Features

Scan Annotation

View the 3D scan along with your central fabricators, annotating areas of interest and collaborating on producing the device.

Messaging

Don’t play phone-chase with your central fabricators to get your questions answered. Discuss your order directly and anytime through messaging.

Patient Tracking

Model your patient’s progress, updating information, analyzing 3D models, and managing media including scans, photos, videos, etc.

Order Tracking

Track your order from start to finish with order statuses, and get an ETA from your central fabricator whenever you like.

Scanner Compatible

CAST is compatible with all 3D scanners on the market that produce standard STL or OBJ files.

Custom Solutions

Choose your favorite central fabricator and fill out a custom order form for the particular device you want.
