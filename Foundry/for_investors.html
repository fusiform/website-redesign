Our Team - Summary

Based at Johns Hopkins University, we merge engineers, artists, scientists, and business leaders to create a versatile team fit to disrupt an industry.

Co-Founders
Param Shah - CEO

Param founded the Lotus Life Foundation, an international 501(c) 3 nonprofit, in 2013. This foundation provides orthotic/prosthetic treatment to approximately 50 children per month in order for them to gain the ability to walk. Param has considerable experience building distribution pipelines for orthotic/prosthetic devices in developing areas, has built the Lotus team from 2 to 17, and has brokered international partnerships with large hospitals and nongovernmental organizations to provide Lotus Life’s services in three states of India. While managing device production and delivery in India, Param has witnessed first-hand the consequences of the industry’s inefficiencies.

Alex Mathews - CTO

Alex has worked in the Johns Hopkins Applied Physics Laboratory for 2 years, where he has developed computer integrated surgical systems for cranioplasty procedures. His research in the laboratory has utilized 3D scanning systems for craniofacial reconstruction during brain surgery. Exposure to these scanning systems has propelled Alex into acquiring the qualifications and skill set necessary to build Fusiform’s software (CAST). Before Alex co-founded Fusiform, he served as the CTO of Jama Cocoa - a chocolate-making startup also founded out of Johns Hopkins. Alex overlooks all software and hardware development of Fusiform.
