Fusiform's Mission

Exponentially faster device delivery,
through collaborative processes.

The assessment and manufacturing processes of the orthopedic device industry haven't progressed in decades. People wait a month to receive an excessively expensive device, only to outgrow it 6 months later.

These devices should give people mobility and confidence, not slow them down. So we're changing it up.

Our technology will get you a device in hours, not weeks, making sure you have your best foot forward sooner than later.

Fusiform joins NY-based Human Condition Labs

May 18, 2016

Human Condition Labs is an NY-based applied research facility that leverages technology to create systems and solutions with large-scale social impact. With a diverse and cross-disciplinary team -- encompassing technology, science, business strategy, media, design, and engineering expertise -- HC Labs bridges industries and partners with a broad array of innovation leaders, such as Autodesk Research and Microsoft. Using a 90-day methodology, HC Labs has applied this process of program management to a range of projects: providing cardiologists with a simulation and understanding of heart failure, partnering with nonprofits to limit the burden of outreach, and crowdsourcing insights on a redesign of the NYC taxi.

Peter Raymond, CEO of Human Condition Global and Founder of HC Labs, serves on Fusiform's Board of Advisors. In conjunction with HC Labs, Fusiform presented it's technology at Exponential Manufacturing 2016, held by Singularity University. Following this presentation, Fusiform has joined HC Labs, collaborating on design and commercialization of our products.

Fusiform at Exponential Manufacturing Conference, held by Singularity University

May 12, 2016

Exponential Manufacturing convened a select group of 400 leading executives, investors, and entrepreneurs from around the globe in Boston to explore how exponential technologies are changing the way humanity designs, makes, produces, moves, sells, and consumes the material world.
